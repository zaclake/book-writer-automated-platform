## Hook & Promise Reinforcement

**Purpose**: Ensure every chapter creates forward pull and the opening delivers a clear story promise.

---

## Chapter 1 Promise

By the end of Chapter 1, the reader must know:
- the **core promise** of the story world,
- the **primary tension** or problem that will drive the narrative,
- the **personal stake** for the protagonist.

Failure Trigger: Chapter 1 ends without an explicit promise or tension.

---

## Chapter Exit Hook

Every chapter must end with a **specific unresolved obligation**, such as:
- a commitment the protagonist must fulfill,
- a threat they must confront,
- a revealed mystery that requires action.

Failure Trigger: Chapter ends with “determination only” or vague intent.

---

## Escalation Micro-Ladder

Each chapter must add at least one:
- **new risk** (danger, loss, exposure), or
- **new opportunity** (ally, leverage, insight).

Failure Trigger: Chapter repeats previous stakes without escalation.

---

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Chapter 1 promise is explicit by final paragraph.
- [ ] Chapter ends with a specific unresolved obligation.
- [ ] New risk or opportunity introduced.
