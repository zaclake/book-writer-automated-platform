## Show-vs-Summary Balancer

**Purpose**: Prevent abstract narration from dominating the chapter and enforce action-grounded prose.

---

## Summary Ratio Cap

- No more than **one** summary paragraph between active beats.
- No more than **two** summary paragraphs in a row in any chapter.

Failure Trigger: three or more consecutive abstract paragraphs without action or sensory grounding.

---

## Emotion-by-Action Requirement

If an emotion is stated, it must be grounded within **two sentences** by:
- a physical action, or
- a sensory cue, or
- a concrete behavioral choice.

Failure Trigger: emotion statements without grounding.

---

## Internal Monologue Limits

- Internal monologue should not exceed **25%** of chapter length unless genre-justified.
- Abstract introspection must be broken by action beats.

---

## Banned Patterns (Unless Justified)

- “He felt/She felt/They felt” more than **once per 800 words**.
- Multi-paragraph exposition without a present-time action.

---

## Verification Checklist

- [ ] Summary ratio within limits.
- [ ] Emotions grounded by action/sensory cues.
- [ ] Internal monologue within capped range.
- [ ] No extended exposition without present-time movement.
